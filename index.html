Product designer

I'm Miguel, a senior product designer based in Bali. I currently work designing for early-stage startups/founders through my own design studio called 96.


For the last 12 years, I've dedicated my life to building and designing software around people's needs. I love crafting products that are pleasant to use and look at.


If you're a founder or startup looking to consult regarding product design, please do reach out via email.
